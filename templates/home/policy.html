Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: January 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better news services to all our readers. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Reading history and interests
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Personalizing your news feed and content recommendations
  • Sending newsletters and updates you've subscribed to
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

Important Note: We never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Site functionality Session
Analytics Performance monitoring 1 year
Preferences User settings 6 months

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications
  • Right to data portability

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

We regularly review and update our security practices to ensure the highest level of data protection.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
